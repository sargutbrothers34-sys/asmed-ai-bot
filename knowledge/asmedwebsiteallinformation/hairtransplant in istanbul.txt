How is Hair Transplant Applied in Istanbul?
Hair transplants in Istanbul typically involve advanced techniques such as FUE Hair Transplant (Follicular Unit Extraction) or Direct Hair Implantation (DHI). The process begins with a consultation to assess the patient's hair loss and goals. Local anesthesia is administered, and hair follicles are extracted from the donor area and carefully implanted into the recipient areas. Skilled surgeons in modern clinics perform the procedure, ensuring precision and natural-looking results.
