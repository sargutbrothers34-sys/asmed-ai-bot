How is Hair Transplant Applied in Turkey?
In Turkey, hair transplants are commonly performed using advanced techniques such as Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Direct Hair Implantation (DHI). The process starts with a consultation to assess the patient’s hair loss and develop a tailored treatment plan. The donor and recipient areas are then prepared and numbed with local anesthesia. Hair follicles are carefully extracted from the donor area using specialized tools and then meticulously implanted into the thinning or balding areas. This procedure is carried out by skilled surgeons in state-of-the-art clinics, ensuring high precision and natural results
