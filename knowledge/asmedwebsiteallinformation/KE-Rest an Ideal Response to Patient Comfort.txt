KE-Rest
An Ideal Response to Patient Comfort


The KE-Rest was developed by Dr. Koray Erdogan to assure patient comfort and support during FUE procedures. Its ergonomic design sustains proper positioning for the patient and the surgeon during long and tedious FUE surgeries while allowing easy access to both donor and recipient areas with relaxation and stress-point relief.
